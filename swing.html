Swingmate

Automated Interactive Golf Coach

Swingmate is an automated driving-range training tool for amateur golfers, enabling them to monitor their performance in real-time. Our solution expands standard facility of the driving range post through in-built sensory measurement system. Golfer's swing is assessed in terms of speed and impact angle. Further, the appropriate body balance is assessed through tensometric measurements under player's feet.

Swingmate has been designed in close cooperation with top Polish players and coaches, as well as amateur adepts of golf. Our lead advisor - Amanda Majsterek, 4-time Polish Champion (Women Open) and professional coach - enabled us to deeply understand the problems of amateur players and advised us on the design of the solution.

Swingmate gathers three different types of data:

  • the speed of the club, measured just befor impact
  • the angle between the perpendicular impact line and the club face
  • tensometric measurment on player's feet balance
and analyses the swing in real time, providing feedback on the registered parameters right after the shot. The correctness of feet balance is presented live, through a LED display between player's feet. Therefore, players can improve their technique in every shot.

Pilot studies on appropriate visualisation techniques has been performed. Currently, our team is working on application of new display methods and improving the performance of measurement system. When finished, a longitudinal study in the golf club is to be performed, to verify whether the device does have significant impact on progress made by the amateurs.
