Ongoing Projects

Swingmate - Automated Interactive Golf Coach

UbiCOMP Engineering Club
Lodz University of Technology

April 2018 - present

Swingmate is a system for automatic golf swing analysis tool, designed to support training routines of amateur players. This interactive driving range post measures basic parameters of the swing, providing real-time feedback on shot quality. Swingmate was designed hand-in-hand with top Polish professional players and coaches, to offer tailored support for beginners.

Mixed Reality Applications for Amblyopia Therapy

UbiCOMP Engineering Club
Lodz University of Technology

January 2018 - present

We explore the potential of stereoscopic displays of Microsoft HoloLens in curing the lazy eye syndrome among children. Mixed reality technology enables precise control the display projected to each of the user's eye, which can be employed for appropriate stimulation of the disabled vision apparatus. We are looking for solutions which will not only aid the casual treatment, but also reduce the troublesome aspects of the casual therapeutical approach.

Interactive Tools for ECT Data Analysis

Institute of Applied Computer Science
Lodz University of Technology

June 2017 - February 2018

Electrical capacitance tomography is one of the most compelling techiques within process control, especially when closed containers are concerned. We developed a system for real-time analysis of the measurement result for ECT setup, enabling both time-realted and spatial analysis of granular flows. The system was designed for tabletop and tablet operation, broadening the horizons for on-site process control.
